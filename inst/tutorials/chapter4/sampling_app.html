Sampling App

This app simulates the drawing of a random sample of 20 observation from a population, with the underlying data generationg process (DGP):

\[Y = 5 + 2X + \nu, \quad \text{with:}\] \[X \sim \mathcal{N}(5, 2^2)\quad \text{and} \quad \nu \sim \mathcal{N}(0, 5^2)\]

(so \(\nu\) is the sampling variability, or noise)
